Continuing Professional Development [CPD] Certified

88% 0f employers believe that Professional Certificates strengthen a candidate’s job application. Earning a Continuing Professional Development (CPD) certificate endorses your commitment to ongoing lifelong learning.

genconnectU is proud to partner with CPD UK to offer certified courses led by best in class experts and recognized globally. Our courses are designed to provide individuals with the job ready skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their careers. By partnering with CPD UK, we are able to deliver high-quality courses that are recognized and respected by employers around the world. CPD certificates endorse a professional’s efforts to learn something new, improve existing skills or simply keep up to date with the latest developments within a particular profession or industry.

We believe that everyone should have access to affordable and relevant professional development opportunities, and our partnership with CPD UK helps us achieve that goal. genconnectU is also a strong advocate of gender equality and diversity in the workplace.
